Front-end Development

Front-end development can be split up into three major coding langauges: 

HTML - HyperText Markup langauge defines the structure of a website semantically
and the content tha tiwll be rendered by the web broswer. 

CSS - Cascading Stylesheets deal with the presentaiton of content including 
aspects such as layout, formatting and colors.

Javascript -One of the most popular coding langauges in the world, brings
movement and user interation to websites

This curriculur focuses on teh first two cornerstones of modern front-end
